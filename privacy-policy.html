Privacy Policy

1. Who We Are

We are a UK sole trader providing kitchen fitting, bathroom installation, carpentry, plastering, tiling, and general property maintenance services.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect and use your personal information.

2. Information We Collect

  • Name
  • Address
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Property and job details
  • Any information you provide when contacting us

3. How We Collect Information

  • Phone calls, emails, messages, or contact forms
  • Quote requests
  • During work or ongoing communication

4. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide quotes and carry out work
  • To communicate about your project
  • To issue invoices and manage payments
  • To maintain business and legal records

5. Legal Basis for Processing

  • Contract – to deliver services requested
  • Legal obligation – tax and accounting records
  • Legitimate interest – running our business

6. How We Store Your Information

  • Secure, password-protected devices and email accounts
  • Safe storage of any paper records (if used)
  • Reasonable steps to prevent unauthorised access

7. Sharing Information

We do not sell your data.

We may share information only when necessary with:

  • Subcontractors involved in your project (e.g. electricians, plumbers)
  • Accountants for tax purposes
  • Legal or regulatory authorities if required by law

8. How Long We Keep Data

  • Customer records: up to 6 years (tax/legal requirements)
  • Unsuccessful enquiries: up to 12 months
  • After this period, data is securely deleted

9. Your Rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion (where legally possible)
  • Object to processing in certain cases
  • Request a copy of your data

10. Cookies

This website may use basic cookies to support functionality and understand usage. You can disable cookies in your browser settings.

11. Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your data, including secure devices, restricted access, and safe storage practices. However, no system is completely secure.

12. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. The latest version will always be available on this page.
